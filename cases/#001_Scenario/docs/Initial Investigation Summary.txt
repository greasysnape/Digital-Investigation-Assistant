2. Summary of Alleged Offence
The suspect is alleged to have violated the Act on Prevention of Divulgence and Protection of Industrial Technology and the Criminal Act (Occupational Breach of Trust). Internal auditing systems at NeoSemi Co., Ltd. flagged unauthorized access to the "Project AEGIS" server—containing proprietary 5nm Power Management IC (PMIC) circuit designs—between February 16 and February 18, 2026. The suspect is believed to have exfiltrated these trade secrets using a personal external storage device and a web-based email client to facilitate a move to a competitor based in Southeast Asia.
